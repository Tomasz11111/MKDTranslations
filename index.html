High standards

  • I provide professional advice and assistance to clients in determining their translation needs;
  • I meet specified deadlines (within reasonable limits);
  • I advise the client in a timely manner when deadlines cannot be met and negotiates new terms;
  • I use consistent terminology in all translations (efficient use of CAT tools and glossaries);
  • I coordinate services if documents require translation into a language other than Polish or proofreading.

Guidelines

  • To ensure a satisfactory level of service, MKD Translations prefers that all documents for translation be provided electronically.
  • However, mailed or couriered documents are accepted.
  • It is difficult for MKD Translations to provide translation of isolated words as meaning depends on context. In order to translate independent terms accurately, MKD Translations requires background information.

Pricing

Transparent pricing policy

MKD translations usually charges on a per-word basis. Clients should bear in mind that the cost of translation may be influenced by many factors such as urgency, technicality of the text, total volume, one-time translation or ongoing project, etc.

Surcharges

MKD translations may add a 10-30 % surcharge for PowerPoint presentations, .htm/html (.php, .xml) files or documents delivered as scanned copies.
Proofreading, DTP services or any kind of cross-cultural consultancy are charged additionally.

Various payments methods

After the completion of your project, MKD translations issues an invoice due within 14 days net (unless otherwise agreed). The preferred payment methods are either a bank transfer to our Eurozone, Polish or Czech bank accounts or payments via Paypal/ Moneybookers
